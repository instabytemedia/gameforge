# Design Brief

## Style: Digital Dungeon

The design should transport users to a futuristic world of board game creation. It should have a dark and moody atmosphere, with neon accents and a focus on digital elements. The layout should be sleek and modern, with a emphasis on the AI-powered tools and game creation features. The overall picture should be a blend of sci-fi and fantasy elements, with 3D illustrations of board games and gaming accessories.

## Design Tokens

| Aspect | Direction |
|--------|-----------|
| Colors | Dark and neon colors |
| Typography | Bold and futuristic typography |
| Components | Angular buttons and cards with a metallic texture |
| Imagery | 3D illustrations of board games and gaming elements |

## Theme Settings

- **Theme Color:** blue
- **Mode:** dark
- **Border Radius:** 0.3

## App Variant: GameForge

> Craft Games, Unite Community

GameForge is a premium platform where board game enthusiasts can design, play, and share their own games, leveraging AI-powered tools for game balancing and mechanics suggestions. It offers a vast library of user-generated games, fostering a community that collaborates and competes. By focusing on user-generated content and community engagement, GameForge creates a unique and constantly evolving experience.

**Target Audience:** Hobbyist game designers and enthusiasts
**Monetization:** Subscription-based with in-app purchases for premium features and game assets
**Complexity:** premium
